About Our Church

Growing Together in Faith, Hope and Love

⛪ Who We Are

ACK St. Paul's Parish Mafisini is a welcoming Anglican church dedicated to worshipping God, preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ, and serving the community through faith, love, and compassion. We provide a spiritual home where believers grow in faith and experience God's presence.

🙏 Our Mission

To proclaim the Good News of Jesus Christ, disciple believers, promote Christian values, and serve our community through worship, fellowship, evangelism, and outreach ministries.

🌍 Our Vision

To become a Christ-centered church that transforms lives, builds strong Christian families, empowers young people, and positively impacts society through faith and service.

📖 Our History

ACK St. Paul's Parish Mafisini continues to grow as a place of worship, prayer, and community service. Through God's grace, the parish spreads the Gospel while strengthening believers through worship services, Bible study, youth programs, fellowships, and outreach activities.

Our Core Values

✝ Faith

Growing deeper in our relationship with God through Jesus Christ.

❤️ Love

Showing God's love through kindness, care and compassion.

🤝 Service

Serving God and humanity with humility and dedication.

🕊 Unity

Building one family of believers in Christ.
